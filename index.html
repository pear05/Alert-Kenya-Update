About Us

Brief Introduction

At Alert Kenya, we are committed to enhancing disaster response and recovery through in-depth research and data-driven solutions. Since our founding in 2024, we have partnered with local governments and organizations to assess disaster impacts, identify critical needs, and develop innovative strategies. Guided by our core values of commitment, collaboration, innovation, and empathy, we strive to support communities in rebuilding and preparing for future challenges. Join us in our mission to create safer, more resilient communities.
